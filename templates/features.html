VaultSync Feature Guide

Every feature, what it does, and how to use it correctly.

Macro Dashboard

Use the dashboard to view monthly spending, budget, projected savings, and the category chart.

Choose month/year at the top. Only expenses from that selected month and your family are included.

Daily Expense Diary

Quickly add small everyday expenses. These are the same expenses that roll into the dashboard.

Use Previous Day, Today, and Next Day to review a focused chronological ledger.

Expected Monthly Expenditure

Write monthly bills in advance. They appear in charts and Recent Activity only after you mark them paid.

Use due day for bill timing, Mark Paid when money actually leaves, Unpost to move it back to upcoming, and Copy This Plan To Next Month for repeat bills.

AI Budget Planner

Export a date or month window as AI-ready JSON, ask an AI to create the next budget, import the JSON answer, compare it with your current plan, then apply it.

The imported AI plan becomes Expected Monthly Expenditure, so it still only affects charts after you mark items paid.

Complete Editing

Expenses, planned expenses, and categories can be edited across all important fields instead of only partial details.

Editing a paid planned expense also updates the linked actual transaction, keeping the dashboard and plan in sync.

Recommended Categories

Preset categories keep spending clean and organized. Add custom categories only when needed.

Recommended categories include groceries, rent/EMI, utilities, transport, healthcare, education, insurance, household, savings, and emergency.

Monthly Archive

Open Archive to browse older months and click any month to review its dashboard.

The archive is built from real expenses, including paid budget-plan items.

Admin Panel

Family managers can update budget, members, and family settings.

The admin panel also shows member spending, planned upcoming totals, saved AI suggestions, category limits, preset category repair, and invite-code regeneration.

Dark Mode and Hindi

Dark Mode and Language controls live at the bottom of the sidebar. Your choice is saved in this browser.
